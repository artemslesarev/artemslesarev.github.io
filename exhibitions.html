4th june 1972

The cube-shaped construction, made of four screens, will allow guests to be in the room of the Brodsky family on the day when Joseph Brodsky left there for good. "June 4, 1972" is built at the intersection of modern technology and theatre, the virtual environment is based on Mikhail Isaevich's photographs and memorial objects.

Vacuum @ Krasnoholmskaya gallery, Moscow 2022

8-channel piece 'Vacuum' was presented as a part of "Razhzhizhenniy Dom" ("Liquified house") exhibition. Multichannel audio based on Navier-Stokes algorithm as a control source and granular synthesis as an audio core.
