CV


Artem Slesarev is a sound artist, educator and software audio instruments developer from moscow, ru currently based in tbilisi, ge

His approach highly involves modern computer music techniques, creative coding and field recordings to establish subtle yet affective forms in sonic spaces whether it's a composition or a software itself that creates audible experience.

Currently, as an artist he develops an unitary generative system for live performances and doing research in an AI and ecology field, and its ability to interconnect

As a software engineer he started cores. Under its moniker he develops audio software. His last collaborative work with Pavel Milaykov is a sampler Quantum the entire income from which goes to various chartiy/volounteer organizations based in Ukraine.

As an educator he is teaching Max/MSP in person and as invited lecturer at soundartist.ru He also had an experience as a lecturer at HSE @ 2021-2022

As a sound designer he mostly works on a cinema/ads/live events post production. His works in this field can be seen here

As a guitar player he took part in the last Boiler Room that perhaps took place in Moscow as a part of a group Son Acido. After russian invasion band splited up.
